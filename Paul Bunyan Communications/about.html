Who We Are

Paul Bunyan Communications is dedicated to improving quality of life through advanced telecommunications.

Our Cooperative Story

Founded in 1952, Paul Bunyan Communications is a member-owned cooperative serving Northern Minnesota. Our mission is to provide reliable, future-ready communication services to rural communities.

Our Mission

We exist to connect our members with technology that empowers education, business, healthcare, and everyday life.

  • Innovation through fiber technology
  • Member-first service
  • Long-term sustainability
